Frequently Asked Questions

Are custom PC builds tested before delivery?

Yes, every single custom rig undergoes a rigorous 24-hour stress test (Prime95 & FurMark) and thermal benchmarking before shipping.

Do laptops come with official manufacturer warranty?

100%. All laptops sold through Rajiv Computers carry standard 1-to-3 years comprehensive brand warranty valid across India.

Can I modify components in pre-built rigs?

Absolutely! You can upgrade RAM, storage, or request specific liquid-cooling loops directly through our support team or admin channel.

What are the shipping timelines?

Standard components ship within 24-48 hours. Custom built water-cooled rigs require 3-5 business days for assembly and cable management.
